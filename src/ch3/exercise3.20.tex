We shall perform a contour integral of $f(z) := \inv{{\left( a + bz^2 \right)}^3}$ along \reffig{}~\ref{fig:problem3-20}.
\[
	\oint_C dz f(z)
	= 2i\pi \res{z=i\sqrt{\frac{a}{b}}} f(z)
	= \frac{2i\pi}{2!} \cdot \evalAt{\frac{d^2}{dz^2} \inv{b^{3/2} {\left( \sqrt{b}z + i\sqrt{a} \right)}^3}}{z=i\sqrt{\frac{a}{b}}}
	= \frac{3\pi}{8 a^{5/2} b^{1/2}}
\]
We also have
\[
    \oint_C dx f(z)
    = \int_{-R}^{R} \frac{dx}{{\left( a + bx^2 \right)}^3}
     + \int_0^{\pi} d\theta \frac{iRe^{i\theta}}{{\left( a + bR^2 e^{2i\theta} \right)}^3}
    \xrightarrow{R \rightarrow \infty} 2\int_0^{\infty} \frac{dx}{{\left( a + bx^3 \right)}^3}.
\]
\[
	\therefore \int_0^{\infty} \frac{dx}{{\left( a + bx^3 \right)}^3} = \frac{3\pi}{16 a^{5/2} b^{1/2}}
\]
